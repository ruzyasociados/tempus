Transfer pricing studies (Moody's Analytics (Orbis))

Unmatched Coverage of Private Companies

Orbis' main strength is its powerful database of private companies worldwide, with information on hundreds of millions of companies around the world. This is crucial for transfer pricing analyses, as private companies are often the best comparables, but also the most difficult to find.

Specific Functionality for Transfer Pricing

Orbis integrates features that enable users to plan and establish transfer pricing policies, manage risks, and document procedures to comply with regulations. It includes preconfigured search templates that guide users through the process of selecting comparable companies.

Analysis of Corporate Structures

A notable feature is the ability to visualize complex ownership structures and corporate trees. This is critical for identifying and ruling out related parties, an essential step in any comparability analysis.

Data Quality and Standardization

The platform strives to standardize the financial statements of companies from different countries to facilitate direct comparison.

Additional Modules

It offers complementary modules such as TP Catalyst, a guided solution for the entire transfer pricing workflow, and access to databases of royalties and merger and acquisition (M&A) transactions.
