LSEG (formerly Refinitiv/Thomson Reuters with Eikon, now Workspace)

Strength in Public Companies

Its strength lies in the depth and quality of financial data on companies listed on stock markets around the world.

Integrated Financial Analysis Tools

LSEG Workspace provides an ecosystem of analysis, news, and market data. Its integration with Microsoft allows data feeds and analysis to be incorporated directly into tools such as Teams and other Office 365 applications.

Search engine for specific documents and data

Allows you to perform detailed searches in annual company reports (such as Forms 10-K and 20-F) to find business descriptions and confirm functional comparability.

Data for Financial Transactions

It is particularly useful for analyzing intra-group financial transactions, such as loans, as it has databases of debt agreements (Dealscan) and market interest rates. It also includes royalty rate modules for the analysis of intangibles.

Real-Time Data and News

It offers access to breaking news from sources such as Reuters, allowing real-time market tracking.
