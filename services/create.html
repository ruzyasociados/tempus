Multinational companies may decide to establish a holding company for a number of reasons. For example, a holding company can be an efficient vehicle for owning shares in other companies to form a corporate group, to allow risk reduction for owners and thus enable ownership and control of several different companies, or, among other things, to provide tax efficiency in relation to withholding taxes on dividends and capital gains taxes.
