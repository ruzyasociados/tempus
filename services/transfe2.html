Comparative: LSEG Workspace vs S&P Global Market Intelligence (Capital IQ)

Both platforms are global leaders in financial information and company analysis. Here you can compare their main strengths and features to choose the best option based on your needs for analysis, searching for comparables, and accessing market data.

LSEG Workspace

  • Strength in public companies: Depth and quality of financial data for companies listed on global markets.
  • Integrated financial analysis tools: Market analysis, news, and data ecosystem. Integration with Microsoft Teams and Office 365.
  • Search engine for specific documents and data: Detailed searches in annual reports (10-K, 20-F) to confirm functional comparability.
  • Data for financial transactions: Debt agreement databases (Dealscan), market interest rates, and royalty modules for intangibles.
  • Real-time data and news: Access to breaking news (Reuters) and real-time market tracking.

S&P Global Market Intelligence (Capital IQ)

  • Powerful search tool (screening): Allows you to apply dozens of filtering criteria to find comparable companies accurately.
  • Financial data quality: Data depth and standardization, with adjustments and normalizations (Compustat).
  • Integración con Excel: Add-in muy potente para crear modelos financieros dinámicos y actualizar datos en hojas de cálculo.
  • Comparable analysis: Facilitates compliance with obligations and analysis of payments, returns, etc.
  • Comprehensive coverage: Global coverage of public and private companies, with historical strength in North America.

Which one should you choose?

The choice depends on your needs: LSEG is ideal if you are looking for depth in public company data, integration with Microsoft, and financial transaction analysis. S&P stands out for its flexibility in searching for comparables, standardized data quality, and advanced integration with Excel. Both offer global coverage and industry-leading analysis tools.
